FAQs

Frequently asked
questions

Everything you need to know about fertility treatment at LogixX. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

We offer a full range of fertility treatments including IVF (In Vitro Fertilisation), IUI (Intrauterine Insemination), ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection), egg freezing, embryo freezing, sperm freezing, donor egg/sperm programmes, and pre-implantation genetic testing (PGT). Our specialists will recommend the most appropriate treatment after a thorough assessment of your individual circumstances.

A typical IVF cycle takes between 4 to 6 weeks from the start of stimulation to the embryo transfer. However, this can vary depending on your individual protocol. The stimulation phase usually lasts 8–14 days, followed by egg retrieval, fertilisation and embryo development (3–5 days), and then a fresh or frozen embryo transfer. We will give you a precise timeline tailored to your protocol during your initial consultation.

Our overall clinical pregnancy rate is 85%, significantly above the national average of around 32% per embryo transfer. Success rates vary by age and individual factors. For women under 35 using their own eggs, our success rate exceeds 60% per transfer. We are transparent about our statistics and publish our full outcome data annually. During your consultation, we will give you personalised success rate estimates based on your specific situation.

We recommend discussing any supplement with your doctor or our fertility specialist before starting, especially if you are already on medication.

Costs vary depending on the treatment type. An initial consultation is free. A standard IVF cycle starts from $8,500, which includes all monitoring scans, egg retrieval, lab fees, and one fresh embryo transfer. We also offer flexible payment plans, multi-cycle packages with savings, and can help you navigate insurance coverage. We believe financial concerns should never be a barrier to starting your family, and our finance team will work with you to find the best option.

No, you do not need a GP referral to book your initial consultation at LogixX. You can self-refer by booking online or calling our team directly. However, if you have had previous fertility investigations or treatments elsewhere, we would recommend bringing any relevant test results or medical records to your appointment. This helps our specialists give you the most informed and personalised assessment possible.

Your initial consultation is a relaxed, 60-minute appointment with one of our senior fertility consultants. We will review your medical and reproductive history, discuss your goals, and may arrange initial fertility tests such as a hormone blood panel and ultrasound scan. By the end of the appointment, you will have a clear picture of your fertility status and a personalised treatment plan with options, timelines, and costs outlined. There is absolutely no pressure to proceed.

Yes. Elective egg freezing (social egg freezing) is one of our most popular services. We recommend it for women between 25–37 who wish to preserve their fertility for the future. The process involves 10–12 days of hormone stimulation, a brief egg retrieval procedure, and secure long-term storage. Frozen eggs can be stored for up to 10 years. We recommend booking a fertility assessment first so we can advise on the optimal time to freeze based on your ovarian reserve and age.
